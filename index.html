🌌 My Journey

My journey wasn’t a straight one—it was more of a winding path of self-discovery. As a child, I marveled at the idea of flying planes, until I realized that life in the skies wasn’t for me. In secondary school that idea blossomed into a desire to fix planes, fascinated by how things worked behind the scenes.

However as I got older, my curiosity shifted toward understanding how money and businesses operate. That curiosity eventually evolved into a bigger vision: using AI and data to drive change, one project at a time.

I pursued this calling through education—earning a Business Administration degree from Ashesi University, graduating with second-class honours. To deepen my technical expertise, I completed a Master’s in Data Science with Distinction at the University of Essex. My penchant for learning also drove me to acquire the Microsoft Azure Data Science Associate Certification, further solidifying my foundations in modern data solutions.

Every step of this journey—whether it was airplanes, business, or AI—has shaped my perspective. It’s taught me adaptability, focus, and the power of curiosity. Today, I channel that energy into building solutions with data, blending creativity and technical skill to make a real impact.
